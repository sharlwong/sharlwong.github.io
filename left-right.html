Jesus makes some big claims that, if true, have a serious impact on our lives, whether we believe him or not. Who is this Jesus that Christians make such a big deal about? What were his claims?


If you have questions about Jesus or Christianity, Just Looking is the place for you. Just Looking is a 7-week course that explores the claims of Jesus through a biography of Jesus’ life.


The course will take place at 2-3.30pm for 7 Sundays starting from 2 Oct 2016. To sign up or for more information, please email Chew Chern at chewchern[at]gmail.com.
